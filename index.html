Project Overview

At "BreakFree," we believe that every child deserves the chance to thrive. Our project focuses on empowering street children in Adigrat, Tigray, Ethiopia, through a holistic approach that includes education, skill development, creative expression, and community support. By addressing both immediate needs and long-term opportunities, we aim to break the cycle of exploitation and poverty, creating lasting change for the children and the community.

Our Sustainable Model

Education & Skill Development

Our educational programs provide foundational literacy, digital literacy, and creative arts training, equipping children with the skills they need to succeed in life.

Income-Generating Activities

We empower children by teaching them valuable skills that can be monetized, such as art creation, content development, and craft production, fostering self-sufficiency.

Health & Family Support

Our health services ensure the physical well-being of the children, while our family support programs aim to strengthen the overall environment in which they live.

Innovative Use of AI

We leverage AI to enhance learning, creativity, and health monitoring, ensuring that every child receives personalized and effective support.
